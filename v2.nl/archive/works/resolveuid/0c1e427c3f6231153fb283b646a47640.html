Artm Baguinski

Артём Багинский is a software engineer. He worked at V2_Lab from 2000 till 2013. V2_ continues a collaboration with him.

Artm helps artists to use computers and makes computers do what artists want. This process involves translation of artistic concepts to hard technology, looking for possible solutions to arising problems and finally implementing, testing and adjusting them by way of writing and customizing software. Artm's interests include linguistics, science, open source software, live generative visuals, learning languages to read in (if they are of humans) or to write programs in (if they are of computers).

Specialties: Development and customization of software for graphics, video processing, sensors and other multimedia.
